## Experience

#### Learning for Life Programme (LLP) – Aesthetics

All students will be able to:

*   Experience varied Art Forms in breadth and depth
*   Develop their interests and abilities

Platforms created will be:

*   Cultural Experience Modules
*   PAL Lessons
*   School-based performances
*   School-level Exhibitions @ Art Gallery
