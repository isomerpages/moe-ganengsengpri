<h5>P6 Trip for International Experience to Thailand</h5><p>Our Primary 6 students embark on an exciting interactive learning journey to discover careers in Singapore's thriving aviation industry. Through this programme, students explore how Changi Airport's workforce contributes to making it one of the world's best airports, deepening their understanding of this significant Singapore landmark. Students will discover various job opportunities across Changi Airport, from Airport Management and Ground Handling to Ground Operations, gaining valuable exposure to occupations in the growing aviation industry. The highlight of the programme is a visit to the Changi Experience Studio, where students enjoy free exploration of Changi Airport's virtual world through interactive games and immersive shows.</p>
