## Choir’s Performance at MOE’s START Award Ceremony

The GESPS Choir was invited to perform at the Singapore Teaching and Academic Research Talent Scheme (START) Awards Ceremony on 14 August 2018. The Guest-of-Honour for the ceremony was Minister for Education, Mr Ong Ye Kung.

Our choir performed a total of 3 songs – 小白船 (xiao bai chuan), Little Red Dot and Home. The students were confident and strived to succeed through the process.

We are proud of our students’ performance!
