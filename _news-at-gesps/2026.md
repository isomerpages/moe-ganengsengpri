Grandparents’ Day is celebrated in the month of November. To celebrate grandparents who reside in the Redhill neighbourhood, NTUC Health Ageing Centre organised a fun-filled morning of activities. Gan Eng Seng Primary School was invited to be part of this celebration to promote intergenerational bonding and community integration. Grandparents living in the vicinity were encouraged to bring their grandchildren with them to spend quality time forging closer ties.

Our GESPians hosted games to engage the guests cognitively and physically in our self-made games. It was indeed a great experience for our Prefects to practise their skills in communication and confidence-building.
