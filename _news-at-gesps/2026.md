<h4>Minister for Education, Mr Desmond Lee Visits GESPS</h4>
<p>GESPS was honored to host the Minister for Education, Mr Desmond Lee,
during his visit to our school. The Minister engaged with students and
staff and observed key learning programmes in action. The visit served
as a valuable platform to showcase our students’ experiences and our commitment
to fostering a vibrant, future-ready environment.</p>

<h4>Celebrating Student Excellence in Sports and the Arts</h4>
<p>Gan Eng Seng Primary School celebrates the outstanding efforts of our students in the National School Games, where they have demonstrated determination, resilience, and a strong competitive spirit. As we prepare for our Singapore Youth Festival (SYF) performances, our students continue to show dedication and passion, working tirelessly to refine their skills and showcase their talents. We are immensely proud of their active involvement, not just in striving for excellence, but in embracing the values of perseverance, teamwork, sportsmanship, and respect for others. Through these meaningful experiences, our students grow not only as performers and athletes, but also as individuals of strong character.

<h4>Co-Create Hub: Where Ideas Come to Life</h4>
<p>We are excited to showcase the Co-Create Hub, a space where learning comes
alive through exploration, creativity, and collaboration.</p>
<p>Here, students design solutions and build prototypes using simple materials,
transforming abstract ideas into tangible reality. By testing and refining
their creations, they develop the critical thinking and resilience needed
to thrive. In this collaborative environment, students learn to communicate
their process and persevere through challenges—growing into confident,
curious learners.</p>
