<h4>Silver Joy @ Jalan Bukit Merah</h4>
<p>As part of our Interest-Based Leadership (Values-in-Action) initiative, 29 student leaders participated in the Silver Joy programme at Jalan Bukit Merah on 2 May 2026.
During this meaningful VIA experience, students engaged elderly residents through a series of thoughtfully designed games aimed at promoting movement and active participation. These activities focused on simple psychomotor skills, encouraging seniors to stay active while creating opportunities for meaningful intergenerational interaction.
Throughout the session, our student leaders demonstrated empathy, confidence, and creativity as they guided and supported the residents. Beyond facilitating the games, students experienced the joy of connecting with the elderly, gaining a deeper understanding of their needs and the importance of building an inclusive and caring community.
This experience allowed our students to lead with purpose and make a meaningful contribution, bringing joy to the residents while also shaping their own growth as compassionate and thoughtful leaders.

<h4>Celebrating Student Excellence in Sports and the Arts</h4>

<h4>Co-Create Hub: Where Ideas Come to Life</h4>
<p>We are excited to showcase the Co-Create Hub, a space where learning comes
alive through exploration, creativity, and collaboration.</p>
<p>Here, students design solutions and build prototypes using simple materials,
transforming abstract ideas into tangible reality. By testing and refining
their creations, they develop the critical thinking and resilience needed
to thrive. In this collaborative environment, students learn to communicate
their process and persevere through challenges—growing into confident,
curious learners.</p>
